Linear Algebra for Engineering Students

At each session, I ask the students to work several problems for the first part of class. During the last part of class I go over how to solve each problem and provide the solution.

Four (4) times during the term the class will include a closed-book quiz. The first of these will be February 2. The remainder will be announced the week prior.

Worked problems from each session

Week 1: Solving using row operations

Week 2: Trace, Transpose, Multiplication, Inverses

Week 3: More Inverses, Determinant

Week 4: More Determinants, Cramer's Rule

Week 5: Dot and Cross Products

Mathematics Help Sessions
