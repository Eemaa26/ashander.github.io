Background

In 2010 I started a PhD at UC Davis in the Population Biology Graduate Group. I'm working with Marissa Baskett and partially funded by the NSF REACH IGERT and Population Biology Graduate Group.

Prior to arriving at UC Davis I was a PIMS IGTC fellow in mathematical biology with Prof. Mark Lewis at the Centre for Mathematical Biology/Department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at the University of Alberta.

After earning a B.Sc. in physics from Stanford University Stanford University in 2004, I spent time as a consultant dealing with intellectual property in the SF Bay area.
